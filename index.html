Our Mission

The Embassy of Lebanon in the United Arab Emirates is dedicated to promoting diplomatic relations, supporting the Lebanese diaspora, and facilitating consular services for Lebanese citizens residing in or visiting the UAE.

  • Issuing and renewing Lebanese passports and Palestinian travel documents
  • Processing visa applications (tourist, business, and household helpers)
  • Providing notarial services and legal document authentication
  • Registering civil status events (birth, marriage, divorce, death) for Lebanese citizens
  • Supporting Lebanese businesses and investors in the United Arab Emirates
  • Promoting cultural and educational exchanges between Lebanon and the United Arab Emirates
