Bilateral Trade

Trade flows, key sectors, and economic ties between Lebanon and the UAE

Bilateral Trade Overview

The UAE is one of Lebanon's leading trade partners in the Arab world. Lebanese exports to the UAE include food products, beverages, jewellery, chemicals, and industrial goods. UAE exports to Lebanon include petroleum products, aluminium, and manufactured goods.

Lebanese Exports to UAE

  • Food and agricultural products
  • Beverages and tobacco
  • Gold jewellery and precious metals
  • Chemicals and pharmaceuticals
  • Machinery and electrical equipment
  • Paper and printing products

UAE Exports to Lebanon

  • Petroleum products and mineral fuels
  • Aluminium and aluminium products
  • Plastics and chemical products
  • Machinery and transport equipment
  • Iron and steel products
  • Manufactured consumer goods

Key Sectors

  • Banking and financial services
  • Hospitality and F&B
  • Real estate and construction
  • Healthcare and pharmaceuticals
  • Technology and professional services
  • Education and training
