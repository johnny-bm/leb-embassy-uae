Visa

Tourist, business, and household helper visas for travel to Lebanon

Important Notice

Visa validity ranges from 7 days to 6 months. Entry visas are not granted to passports bearing an Israeli visa or entry stamp.

Tourist Visa

Required Documents
  • Complete the visa application form
  • 1 passport-size photo
  • Original passport valid for at least 6 months, with one copy
  • UAE residency valid for at least 6 months, with one copy
  • Hotel booking or invitation letter from a Lebanese citizen (include copy of host's passport, full address, and valid contact number)
  • Round-trip ticket booking
  • Bank statement for the last 3 months and salary certificate
  • No Objection Letter from sponsor stating the reason for the visit
  • Copy of trade licence (if applicant is an investor or partner in a business)
  • Valid travel insurance
Courier Service

Business Visa

Required Documents
  • Complete the visa application form
  • 1 passport-size photo
  • Original passport valid for at least 6 months, with one copy
  • UAE residency valid for at least 6 months, with one copy
  • Hotel booking
  • Round-trip ticket booking
  • Bank statement for the last three months and salary certificate
  • No Objection Letter from sponsor stating the reason for the visit
  • Invitation letter from the company stating the reason for the visit
  • Copy of trade licence (if applicant is an investor or partner in a business)
  • Valid travel insurance
  • Family visit: document proving relationship with the person in Lebanon, plus health insurance
Courier Service

Household Helpers Visa

Required Documents
  • Complete the visa application form
  • 1 passport-size photo
  • Original passport valid for at least six months, with one copy
  • UAE residency valid for at least six months, with one copy
  • UAE national sponsor: NOC from Emirati sponsor stating the helper accompanies the Lebanese family, plus copy of sponsor's passport
  • Lebanese citizen sponsor: Copy of Lebanese sponsor's passport or spouse's passport (if she is Lebanese). Helper may obtain a free visa on arrival at Rafik Hariri International Airport when accompanied by the sponsor or their immediate family
  • Company sponsor: NOC from the company stating the helper accompanies the Lebanese family, plus passport copy of the accompanying family member
