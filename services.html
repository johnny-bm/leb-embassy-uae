Consular Services

Official consular services for Lebanese citizens and residents in the UAE

Before Your Visit

  • Opening hours: Monday to Thursday, 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM. The Embassy is closed on Fridays, Saturdays, Sundays, and Lebanese and UAE public holidays.
  • Documents: Bring originals and at least two photocopies of every required document. Incomplete submissions cannot be processed.
  • Fees: All fees are quoted in UAE Dirhams (AED) and are payable on-site. See the individual service page for the applicable fee schedule.
  • Appointments: Passport and civil status services require an appointment. Walk-in enquiries are welcome during opening hours.
  • Location: Villa 10, Street 14, Sector W10-02, Khalidiyah, Abu Dhabi — View map & directions

Lebanese Passports

New biometric passports, renewals, newborn registration, electronic (2003) passports, laissez-passer, and damaged document replacement.

View Requirements

Civil Status

Birth, marriage, divorce, and death registrations. Citizenship by marriage, religious conversion, and other civil status transactions.

View Requirements

Power of Attorney

Notarised powers of attorney for real estate, vehicles, banking, inheritance, company affairs, divorce, customs, and general purposes.

View Requirements

Certificates & Attestations

Embassy-issued certificates (life, residence, singleness, and more) and attestation of documents issued in Lebanon or the UAE.

View Requirements

Visa

Tourist, business, and household helper visas for travel to Lebanon. Single, double, and multiple-entry options available.

View Requirements

Palestinians Travel Document

Travel document issuance and renewal for Palestinian refugees registered in Lebanon. DHL fast-track delivery available.
