Power of Attorney

Notarised powers of attorney for Lebanese nationals and Palestinian holders of Lebanese travel documents

Essential Requirements for All POA Types

This service is processed exclusively for Lebanese nationals or Palestinians holding a Lebanese travel document residing in the UAE. Foreign nationals must use a UAE Notary Public (attested by the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs) before submission. The principal must appear in person with a valid original Lebanese passport or Lebanese ID (plus 1 copy) and a copy of UAE residency. An identification copy of the agent's passport or Lebanese ID is also required. For custom POA text, send a Word document to notary@lbembassy.com.

Additional Documents by Type

Real Estate (sale or purchase)

True or original copy of the property deed or registry certificate (Ifada Ikariya) — dated no more than 5 days prior to application.

Vehicle

Original vehicle licence.

Banking

Copy of bank account details showing account number, bank name, branch, and applicant's name — or POA text provided by the bank.

Divorce & Marriage

Copy of spouse's/divorcee's passport or Lebanese ID, and name of the relevant competent authorities.

Inheritance shares

True or original copy of probate court decision.

Company establishment

Copy of company documents and information.

Revocation

Clear copy of the POA being revoked, the agent's residence address, contact phone number, and ID.

Customs pledge — register car in Lebanon
  • Original passport with 1 copy
  • Copy of UAE residency
  • Copy of UAE car licence
  • Copy of customs statement in Lebanon showing car data
