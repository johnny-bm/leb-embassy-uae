Certificates & Attestations

Embassy-issued certificates and authentication of documents issued in Lebanon or the UAE

All documents submitted for authentication must be stamped by either the Lebanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs & Emigrants or the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Present your original passport, a copy, and a copy of your UAE residency for all services.
