Palestinians Travel Document

Issuance and renewal for Palestinian refugees holding Lebanese travel documents

Who is eligible

The Embassy issues and renews Palestinian travel documents for Palestinians holding Lebanese travel documents residing in the UAE. The applicant's presence is mandatory. Minors (under 18): both parents must be present. In cases of divorce, annulment, or death, the legal guardian must present a guardianship/custody ruling executed in Lebanon (valid ≤ 1 year) or a Power of Attorney (valid ≤ 3 months).

Delivery Options

Diplomatic Pouch: 6–9 weeks. You will receive an SMS when the document is ready to collect.  |  DHL Fast Track: 4–5 weeks. Apply at the Embassy or via fasttracklb.dhl.com. Additional fee: approx. USD 144. Collect one day after receiving DHL confirmation email.

Required Documents

Biometric: 1 copy each  |  Electronic renewal: 2 copies each

  • Original travel document
  • UAE residency
  • Palestinian blue ID card
  • UNRWA refugee card
  • Birth certificate
  • Recent white-background photos (4.3×3.5 cm) — not the same as the travel document photo, not copied from an old photo
  • Lost/stolen document: police report + letter from UAE sponsor confirming the travel document is not in their possession
  • Divorce/annulment/death: guardianship/custody ruling executed in Lebanon (valid ≤ 1 year) or Power of Attorney (valid ≤ 3 months)
