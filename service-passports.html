Biometric Passport

New application or renewal

Required Documents
  • Original passport with 1 copy
  • UAE residency with 1 copy
  • Civil status extract (Ikhraj Kaid Ifrady) not exceeding 10 years, or computerised Lebanese ID — with 1 copy
  • Recent white-background photos (4.3×3.5 cm) — not the same as passport photo, not copied from an old photo
  • Lost/stolen passport: police report + letter from UAE sponsor confirming the passport is not in their possession (2 copies of each)
  • Profession recording: Lebanese document proving profession (union membership card + latest receipt, or commercial broadcast)
Fees

Minors under 18: AED 552 (5-year passport)

Adults 18 and above: AED 1,103 (10-year passport — mandatory)

Electronic Passport (2003)

Renewal only — 1 or 2 years

Lebanese holders of an expired 2003 electronic passport aged 15–59 who have never obtained a biometric passport must travel to Lebanon to apply. The Embassy may renew the electronic passport for 1 or 2 years only, with a written pledge that the holder will obtain a biometric passport upon their next visit to Lebanon.

Required Documents
  • Complete the application form
  • Original passport with 2 copies
  • UAE residency with 2 copies
  • Civil status extract (Ikhraj Kaid Ifrady) not exceeding 10 years, or computerised Lebanese ID — with 2 copies
  • 2 recent white-background photos (4.3×3.5 cm)
  • Divorce cases: guardianship ruling issued from Lebanon, or Power of Attorney (max 3 months validity)
Fees

1 year: AED 111

2 years: AED 221

Courier Service

Laissez-Passer

Emergency travel document to Lebanon — AED 111

Valid for travel to Lebanon only, for a period of 1–3 months.

Laissez-Passer for Newborn

Issued after registering the newborn. Both parents must be present.

  • Complete the application form
  • Original local birth certificate certified by UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs, with 2 copies
  • 2 copies of each parent's passport and UAE residency
  • 2 copies of a Family Extract (Ikhraj Kaid Aa'ili) and Lebanese marriage document executed in Lebanon
  • 3 recent passport photos of the baby
Laissez-Passer — Lost Passport
  • Complete the application form
  • 2 copies of police report (loss must be reported immediately)
  • Letter from UAE sponsor confirming passport is not in their possession (2 copies)
  • 3 recent white-background photos (4.3×3.5 cm)
  • 2 copies of the lost passport
  • 2 copies of UAE residency
  • 2 copies of civil status extract (Ikhraj Kaid Ifrady, max 10 years) or Lebanese ID

Damaged Passport

A new passport may be issued if the damaged passport is biometric, or if the applicant is under 15 or over 60 (regardless of type). Holders of damaged electronic passports must obtain a Laissez-Passer to return to Lebanon and apply for a biometric passport at General Security.

Documents — New Passport
  • Damaged passport (will be sent to Lebanon with application)
  • Clear copy of the damaged passport
  • UAE residency with 1 copy
  • Civil status extract (max 10 years) or Lebanese ID with 1 copy
  • Recent white-background photos (4.3×3.5 cm)
  • Written statement from applicant explaining how the passport was damaged

Fees: AED 552 (5-year)  |  AED 1,103 (10-year)

Documents — Laissez-Passer (electronic passport)
  • Complete the application form
  • 3 recent white-background photos (4.3×3.5 cm)
  • 2 copies of the damaged passport
  • 2 copies of UAE residency
  • 2 copies of civil status extract (max 10 years) or Lebanese ID
