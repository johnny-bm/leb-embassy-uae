Civil Status

Birth, marriage, divorce, and death registrations, plus citizenship and religious conversion

Birth Registration

Free of charge

Registration can be submitted by either parent. The parents' marriage must be registered before registering the child.

Required Documents
  • Complete the birth registration form
  • Official Arabic birth certificate legalised by the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs, with 1 copy
  • 1 copy of each parent's passport and UAE residency
  • Parents' family civil status extract (Ikhraj Kaid Aa'ili), dated less than 1 year, with 1 copy
  • Parents' marriage registration with 1 copy
Courier Service

Marriage Registration

Free of charge

The couple must be present, or the Lebanese party if one spouse is not Lebanese.

Required Documents
  • Complete the marriage registration form
  • Official marriage certificate legalised by the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs, with 1 copy
  • Recent individual civil extract (Ikhraj Kaid Ifradi), dated less than 3 months, with 1 copy
  • 1 copy of each spouse's passport and UAE residency
  • Non-Lebanese spouse: birth certificate from competent authorities in their country (translated into Arabic and certified by UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs) with 1 copy
  • Syrian national spouse: Syrian civil status not exceeding 3 months, with 1 copy
Courier Service

Divorce Registration

AED 232 + 2/1000

Both spouses must be present, or the Lebanese party if one is not Lebanese. One party must arrange a Power of Attorney for a third party in Lebanon to follow up on proceedings.

Required Documents
  • Complete the divorce registration form
  • Final divorce act issued by the relevant UAE court, legalised by the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • 1 copy of each spouse's passport and UAE residency
  • Recent family civil extract (Ikhraj Kaid Aa'ili), dated less than 3 months, with 1 copy
  • Recent authenticated Lebanese marriage certificate (not older than 3 months), with 1 copy
  • Non-Lebanese spouse: birth certificate (translated into Arabic) with 1 copy

Death Registration & Repatriation

Free of charge

Emergency outside working hours: +971 50 219 6333

Death Registration Documents
  • Complete the death registration form
  • Official death certificate legalised by the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs, with 1 copy
  • Copy of the deceased's civil status extract (Ikhraj Kaid Ifradi) and family extract (Ikhraj Kaid Aa'ili)
  • Lebanese passport of the deceased with 1 copy (passport will be cancelled and returned)
Body Repatriation to Lebanon
  • Death certificate issued by UAE Ministry of Health, certified by UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • Deceased's passport (for cancellation)

A transfer report and no-objection certificate for repatriation will be issued, and the body will be handed over to the family.

Acquisition of Lebanese Citizenship by Marriage

AED 184

Required Documents
  • Original Lebanese marriage certificate (valid ≤ 3 months), attested by Lebanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • Recent family extract (Ikhraj Kaid Aa'ili), dated less than 3 months, certified by Lebanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs — 3 copies
  • 3 copies of each spouse's passport and UAE residency
  • Lebanese ID, family extract, or birth certificate of the wife (with legal Arabic translation if in foreign language), showing father's name, mother's name, and surname — 3 copies certified by UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs
  • Syrian wife: civil status register confirming marriage to a Lebanese citizen (dated ≤ 3 months)
  • 3 passport photos of the wife
  • 3 copies of the religious or civil marriage certificate

Religious Conversion

AED 232

The applicant must appear in person with two Lebanese witnesses to sign and submit the application.

Required Documents
  • Original UAE religious certificate issued by a court or religious authority — certified per UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs procedures — original + 3 copies
  • 3 copies of the applicant's passport and UAE residency
  • 3 copies of each witness's passport and UAE residency (both witnesses must be Lebanese nationals)
  • 3 copies of civil status extract (dated ≤ 3 months)

For enquiries, contact: documents@lbembassy.com
