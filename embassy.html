Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary

H.E. Tarek Mneymneh

His Excellency Tarek Mneymneh serves as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Lebanon to the United Arab Emirates.

Ambassador Mneymneh leads the Embassy's mission to strengthen the bilateral relationship between Lebanon and the UAE, support the Lebanese community residing in the country, and facilitate diplomatic, economic, and cultural ties between the two nations.

Ambassador's Message

A message from His Excellency the Ambassador will be published on this page. For enquiries, please contact the Embassy directly.

Consul and Deputy Chief of Mission

Pinella Abdallah

Pinella Abdallah serves as Consul and Deputy Chief of Mission at the Embassy of Lebanon in Abu Dhabi, where she supports diplomatic efforts, oversees consular services, ensures the welfare of Lebanese nationals, and contributes to initiatives that strengthen relations between Lebanon and the United Arab Emirates.

Diplomatic Corps

The Embassy is staffed by a team of diplomatic and consular officers dedicated to serving Lebanese citizens and advancing Lebanon's interests in the UAE.

Consular Section

Handles passport issuance and renewal, civil status registrations, powers of attorney, certificates, and all consular services for Lebanese nationals.

View Services

Political & Economic Affairs

Manages bilateral diplomatic relations, monitors political developments, and supports Lebanon–UAE economic and trade cooperation.

Cultural & Community Affairs

Promotes Lebanese culture, heritage, and arts within the UAE, and provides support and resources for the Lebanese community.

Contact the Embassy

For specific departmental enquiries, please contact the Embassy via phone, email, or the online enquiry form.

Consulate General in Dubai

In addition to the Embassy in Abu Dhabi, Lebanon maintains a Consulate General in Dubai serving Lebanese nationals across Dubai and the Northern Emirates.

Contact & Location

  • Working Hours: Monday–Thursday 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM, Friday 8:00 AM – 11:30 AM
